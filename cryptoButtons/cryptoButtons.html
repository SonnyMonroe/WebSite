    The Mission

    In 2023, the fight for independence and self-sovereignty is being waged through the use of blockchain technology and decentralization. This groundbreaking technology presents us with an opportunity to transform the world around us, creating a future where we are free from the suffocating grip of centralized authoritarians. We can aspire to achieve our dreams without the fear of being subjugated or controlled. As the pioneers of the decentralized future, we recognize that the fight for freedom and individual rights is a formidable challenge. It demands endurance, determination, and the responsibility to stand up for what is right, even in the face of opposition and uncertainty. Therefore, I urge us to seize the opportunity and take up the cause of blockchain technology, carrying it forward with enthusiasm and pride. We can use it to establish a more equitable and just society, where every individual has the power to control their financial destiny and pursue their vision of the American dream. Above all, let us always remember the revolutionary and liberating spirit that drove our founding fathers and that continues to inspire us today.
